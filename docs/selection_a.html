Selecting for halting unpredictability


  • Conway looked for a trio of traits: growth, vanishing, and unpredictability

  • I tried automating the process to look for interesting CCA rules.

  • Rules were selected for yielding low accuracy from a halting predition model (trained fresh in each fitness run)

  • Training the halting prediction model was largely not worth it: any rule that produces both vanishing and growing characteristics tends to be unpredictable. A simple baseline fitness objective of ~50% vanishing/persistence produced gliders easily.

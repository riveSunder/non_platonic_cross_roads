HARLI's clever wave



    The reset wave strategy employed by an evolved Hebbian CA policy for high rewards on a changing center of mass objective.

  • HARLI: Hebbian Automata Reinforcement Learning Improviser

  • Hebbian learning rule neural CA policy evolved for high fitness in a set of 4 Life-like rules with a changing center-of-mass reward wrapper.

  • Learned to toggle all cells "on" to generate waves, and exploited access to env.reset() for high rewards.

  • Waves + reset (changes all cells in env back to 0) yields higher reward than gliders or spaceships (effectively bypassing speed of light)

  • Waves work in all train/test rules; small spaceship only works in 2/4!

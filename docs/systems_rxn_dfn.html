Gray-Scott reaction diffusion system (U-Skate World)


    Non-Platonism with respect to spatial resolution ($\Delta x$) in the genia, glaberish, smoothlife, nca (with lenia kernel), gray-scott reaction-diffusion system. Note that the step size has been modified to meet the Courant-Friedrichs-Lewy condition. I investigated gliders in the U-skate parameter region of the Gray-Scott model ( download (mp4)
